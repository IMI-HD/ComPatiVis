Overview

This is a collection of scientific publications that discuss visualizations techniques concerning time-oriented data patient data for the comparison of single patients to multiple patients or cohorts. It includes 22 literatures which formed the basis for the publication Visualization techniques of time-oriented data for the comparison of single patients to multiple patients or cohort

This tool facilitates different filtering options, which are illustrated in the paper. It includes the medical context of the paper, disease, medical objective, different visualization techniques, and comparisons employed in the paper. It allows the user to select different filters, and the papers which satisfy the criteria are listed at the top in the right panel.
